Pong

Instructions:

You are playing as the left player, use your mouse to control the left paddle.
The paddle is used to bounce the ball back to the opponent (same goes with right player).

  • The closer the ball collides to the paddle's center, the faster its horizontal speed will be.
  • The further the ball collides from the paddle's center, the faster its vertical speed will be
  • The winner is the first person to score 7 points.
  • Click on the center text to start and restart game

    Power-ups:
    Z
    		key: Pulls the right paddle to the left paddle's vertical position
    X
    		key: Makes your paddle larger
    C
    		key: Reverts your paddle back to its original size (after using X key)

    Incoporate Breakout gameplay:
    V
    		key
    Adds a series of blocks in front of the AI's side which replicates the gameplay of the breakout game.
    Like the breakout game, the ball can destroy the blocks through collision but will bounce back.
    (You can only add more blocks after destroying all the blocks present in the game)
